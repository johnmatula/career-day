Why Websites?

I’ve always enjoyed design, and I grew up interested in computers. That said, I stumbled into website building totally by accident. I stick with it because of its ever-evolving state.

👦🏻 A Brief History of Young John

When I was about 7, I was playing around on the family computer and stumbled on a program I had never heard of. It kind of looked like Microsoft Word, except that I could add buttons and toggles and things. This ended up being how I made my first website:

As I kept playing around, I figured out that I was adding HTML to the page (who knew!) and had my first goal of getting those buttons to do something.

🎓 What Helped in Middle and High School

In addition to learning on your own, the most helpful classes are math and science! You’ll be a better critical thinker and will calculate counts and sizes as you design.

♿️➕🧓➕📱 Everyone’s Included

There are plenty of reasons why I enjoy working with websites. If I had to pick one, it would be that well-designed sites can accommodate people of all physical and mental abilities in all situations.
